Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Chicken enchiladas with sour cream sauce are one of my mothers favorite dishes so it had to be added to the list of recipes. Chicken enchiladas are a staple of Tex-Mex here in Texas.

Earnesto's in Frisco, Texas is a great place to get quality chicken enchiladas.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Chicken Breast
  • 1 Medium Onion, Minced
  • 1 Tablespoon of Vegetable Oil
  • 8 - 8 inch Flour Tortillas, Softened
  • 1.5 Cups of Grated Monterey Jack Cheese or 1.5 Cups Mexican Blend Cheese, Divided
  • 1/4 Cup of Butter
  • 1/4 Cup of Flour
  • 1 - 15 oz Can of Chicken Broth
  • 1 Cup of Sour Cream
  • 1 - 4 oz Can Chopped Green Chillies or 2 Fresh Chili Peppers, Minced
