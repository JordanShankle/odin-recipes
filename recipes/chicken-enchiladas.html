Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Chicken enchiladas with sour cream sauce are one of my mothers favorite dishes so it had to be added to the list of recipes. Chicken enchiladas are a staple of Tex-Mex here in Texas.

Earnesto's in Frisco, Texas is a great place to get quality chicken enchiladas.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Shredded Cooked Chicken
  • 6 oz. Softened Cream Cheese
  • 8 8 in. Flour Tortillas
  • 1 15 oz Can of Enchilada Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup of Shredded Mexican Cheese Blend
  • 1 Cup of Chunky Salsa
