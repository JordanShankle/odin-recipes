Greek Chicken

Description

Greek chicken is a delicious and easy meal for anyone to create. Whether you are a culinary expert or a college student looking for an easy but tasty meal; greek chicken is a great choice.

Greek chicken comes with pototaoes to really complete the meal and add a bit of starch to your diet.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs. of Skin-on, Bone-in Chicken Thighs
  • 3 Potatoes, Peeled & Quartered
  • 1/2 Cup of Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Cup of Olive Oil
  • 6 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Tablespoon Dried Oregano
  • 1 Tablespoon Kosher Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Dreid Rosemary
  • 1 Teaspoon Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Pinch of Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Cup of Chicken Broth, Divided
  • 1 Teaspoon Chopped Fresh Oregano, Or to Taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F. Lightly oil a large roasting pan.
  2. Place chicken and potatoes in a large bowl. Add lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, dried oregano, salt, rosemary, black pepper, and cayenne. Toss until chicken and potatoes are evenly coated.
  3. Place chicken pieces skin-side up in the prepared pan. Tuck potato pieces around chicken. Drizzle with 2/3 cup chicken broth. Spoon any remaining marinade from the bowl over chicken and potatoes.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Toss chicken and potatoes, then place chicken skin-side up again.
